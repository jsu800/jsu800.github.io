Background & Experiences

Joseph is an accomplished software architect with over 18 years of experience. He has developed and shipped over 400 software applications across web, mobile, wearable, and connected devices, and worked on highly scalable & extensible REST webservices & architectures for Fortune 100 sites drawing billions of pageviews per month.

Joseph has extensive technical management experience, from shepherding open-source technologies to supporting executive-level strategic planning at the world's largest media conglomerates including Disney, Comcast, and NBCUniversal.

Joseph applies technology to solve problems at scale, taking ideas from inception to launch and delivering working & maintainable software. He regularly imparts his experience to others through active mentoring, coaching, and teaching.
